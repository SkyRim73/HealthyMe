Trust in Allah, but tie your camel.

🌙 STEP 1: Build Your Relationship with Allah

"Whoever fears Allah, He will make a way out for him and provide for him from where he never expected." — Qur'an 65:2-3
  • Pray 5 times daily (no matter what — even if your life feels messy).
  • Start your day with Fajr — it gives barakah to your time.
  • Say dhikr daily (start with: SubhanAllah, Alhamdulillah, Allahu Akbar x33 after prayer).
  • Read Qur'an daily — even 5 verses. Understand its message.
  • Make duʿā — speak to Allah like you mean it. Be real with Him.

⚖️ STEP 2: Clean Your Life from Sins

  • Repent sincerely — say: Astaghfirullah and make intention to stop wrong actions.
  • Cut off haram income (if any) — even if it's hard, Allah replaces it with better.
  • Avoid major sins (interest, backbiting, pornography, etc.).
  • Guard your gaze — start lowering your eyes, it helps clean your heart.

🧠 STEP 3: Level Up Your Mind & Skills

  • Learn something valuable — tech, data (like you're doing), business, or trade skills.
  • Be consistent — learn every day, even if it's 30 minutes.
  • Fix your English if needed — communication = money.
  • Focus on solving real problems — the world pays you to solve problems.

💰 STEP 4: Build Your Rizq (Income) the Halal Way

Dua + Effort = Rizq

Allah provides through your efforts.

  • Try freelancing or remote jobs in your skill (e.g., Power BI, SQL, Alteryx).
  • Avoid laziness — it's one of Shaytan's best traps. Treat your day as a mission.
  • Spend wisely — control lifestyle. Save. Avoid debt. Give zakat and sadaqah (even small amounts).

🤝 STEP 5: Be Strong in Character

  • Be honest — even if it costs you.
  • Keep promises — your word is your contract.
  • Speak kindly — with parents, with friends, with strangers.
  • Stay humble — arrogance ruins everything.

💪 STEP 6: Be Physically & Emotionally Strong

  • Workout — strength is part of being a strong Muslim man.
  • Eat clean — avoid junk, eat Sunnah foods (dates, olives, etc.).
  • Sleep properly — don't waste nights on scrolling.
  • Learn to control emotions — anger, jealousy, lust. It's jihad.

🕌 STEP 7: Serve Others & Live With Purpose

  • Help people — even a smile or advice is sadaqah.
  • Teach what you know — don't keep it to yourself.
  • Think legacy — how will people remember you when you're gone?

✅ DAILY CHECKLIST

🔑 Remember This

  • Success is a process, not a moment.
  • Trust in Allah, but tie your camel — put in the work.
  • It's okay to fall — get back up and keep going.
